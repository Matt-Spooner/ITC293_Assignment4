Communication Skills

Communication skills within the solar industry

Solar energy is a cost-effective method to generate electricity for homes and businesses, however the process of choosing the right solar system is one that requires much consideration.

The business must endeavour to understand the client’s needs through meetings and discussions, from which they may collaborate with staff, external organisations, and stakeholders, to develop a solution that is tailored for the project. For such a process to succeed, communication skills are essential.

"Effective business communication is essential to success in today's work environments." Chan, p. 3, this is why communication skills are vital for professionals working within the solar industry.

I have extensive experience designing, developing and installing solar power systems for residential and commercial clients, through which I have employed exemplary communication skills to deliver quality products that exceed expectations.


My Communication Skills

  • Presenting information to individuals and groups, in person or online.
  • Organising and participating in meetings and discussions with staff, clients, external organisations and stakeholders.
  • Creating written and spoken messages to inform clients about the products and services the company provides.
  • Discussing legal and ethical procedures, rules and regulations with clients, staff, external organisations and stakeholders.
  • Coordinating the activities of employees, departments, and between external organisations and stakeholders.
  • Promoting products and services of the company using a range of mediums in diverse contexts and settings, such as blogs, podcasts and email newsletters.
  • Promoting and maintaining an exemplary professional image of the company, its services and products.
  • Utilising web development skills, including HTML, CSS and Javascript coding, to design and develop websites that promote the company and present information about the company’s services to clients and the public.
