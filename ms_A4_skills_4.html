Project Management Skills

Project management skills within the solar industry

Developing solar power system solutions is not a matter of "one size fits all", each client and business has unique needs and circumstances that must be considered during the design process. For this reason, project managements skills are imperative to the solar industry.

I have extensive experience designing, developing and installing solar power systems for residential and commercial clients, through which I have employed exemplary project management skills to deliver quality products that exceed expectations.


My Project Management Skills

  • Organise and attend meetings with the client to identify the problem.
  • Plan and develop the project schedule.
  • Define project deliverables.
  • Identify tools and resources needed for the project.
  • Educate and train team members.
  • Ensure staff licenses and accreditations are up to date and meet appropriate regulatory and legal standards.
  • Assign tasks to staff, teams or external organisations.
  • Conduct project risk assessments.
  • Monitor project status and progress.
  • Use a variety of communication methods to report project status to clients, staff and stakeholders.
  • Obtain project completion sign-off from client.
